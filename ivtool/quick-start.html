Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Sign Up or Log In – Create your IVContent account or log in to your existing one.
  2. Enter Your Content – Paste your blog, video script, or text into the input box.
  3. Select a Format – Choose what you want to create (e.g., Blog Summary, TikTok Script, Pinterest Caption).
  4. Click 'Repurpose' – Let IVContent work its magic and instantly transform your content.
  5. Review & Edit – Make any tweaks to your generated content in the output box.
  6. Download or Copy – Save your new content as a file or copy it to your clipboard for immediate use.
